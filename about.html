Title: CEO of Body Matrix

Age: 22

Body Matrix is a global technology platform for the African fashion industry, with the customer at its heart. We started Fashpa to champion small businesses, in the belief that the Internet would level the playing field by enabling small enterprises from Africa to leverage innovation and technology to grow and compete more effectively in the domestic and global economies. Fashpa is a platform for African design talent in apparel, accessories, art and home ware to showcase and sell their products to a global fashion-conscious customer. We are building a one stop destination of a dynamic edit of some of the most exciting fashion and accessories from around Africa.

Beautifully Made Things, is the foundation of everything we do. Product-wise, we stock functional pieces for the modern woman with the most premium materials and finest craftsmanship. Lifestyle-wise, we strive to create an online destination that empowers women to celebrate the beauty in everything, ourselves, our culture and each other We exist to empower the women and men who make our products. Welcome to our world of “beautifully made things”
